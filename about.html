About Sabana Herons

Sabana Herons is the Applied Robotics Student Research Seedbed at Universidad de La Sabana. We are a community driven by a passion for technology and a commitment to solving real-world challenges. Through applied research and teamwork, we transform complex theories into functional solutions for the future.

  • Advanced Robotics


    We leverage cutting-edge technologies to push the boundaries of automation. Our projects integrate Robotics, Computer Vision, Artificial Intelligence, and Reinforcement Learning to build intelligent systems capable of adapting to dynamic environments.

  • Strategic Innovation


    We are committed to innovation that matters. Our approach goes beyond theory, focusing on developing practical solutions with a tangible impact on industry and society, ensuring that every project delivers real value and technological progress.

  • Collaborative Team


    Our strength lies in our diversity. We are a multidisciplinary team uniting students from various engineering fields and backgrounds. We foster a collaborative environment where distinct perspectives converge to solve complex problems creatively.

Our Mission

The mission of the Applied Robotics Student Research Seedbed at Universidad de La Sabana is to develop innovative robotic solutions with tangible impact. This is achieved through robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence projects aimed at addressing the real needs of society, industry, and the environment. The group promotes applied research, hands-on learning, and interdisciplinary work, training students who are committed to innovation and social impact

Our vision

By 2028, the Applied Robotics Student Research Seedbed at Universidad de La Sabana will consolidate its position as a national benchmark, recognized for the excellence of its technological solutions and the training of comprehensive professionals. We will stand out for transforming the environment through applied research and knowledge transfer, addressing industry and societal challenges with a strong sense of social responsibility.
