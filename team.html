Meet Our Team

Behind every breakthrough, a member of our crew is involved. The team is not only focused on deploying solutions to industrial constraints, but in applying their passion and knowldege to different scopes of society. Diversity is our strength: From mechanical engineers to data scientist, each member provides an insighftull perspective to innovation and desing of each solution. Led by faculty mentors, the combination between academic excellence and creativity creates a new mosaic in which problems are not seen as obstacles, but as steps to our ambitious projects of life.
