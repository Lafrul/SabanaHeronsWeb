Applied Robotics Unisabana

We are the Applied Robotics Student Research Group at Universidad de La Sabana. Our focus goes beyond theory: we are dedicated to developing innovative solutions in robotics, automation, and AI that address real-world challenges. We train the next generation of engineers through hands-on research and projects with tangible social and industrial impact.

Our mission

What is Sabana Herons?

Sabana Herons is the competitive identity of our research group. We believe that competitions are more than just games; they are rigorous testbeds for innovation. By pushing our robots to their limits in global arenas like RoboCup, we accelerate technological development, validating our algorithms and hardware in high-pressure environments. For us, every match is an opportunity to refine autonomous systems that can one day solve complex problems in the real world.
